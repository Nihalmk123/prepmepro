Frequently Asked Questions

What programs does PrepMe.Pro offer?
PrepMe.Pro offers comprehensive college preparation programs including JEE/NEET Foundation courses, university partnership programs, and specialized workshops. We have 8 foundation courses and partnerships with 2 major universities to provide you with structured, expert-led guidance throughout your academic journey.
How do I get started with PrepMe.Pro?
Getting started is simple! You can begin by registering for one of our free workshops to see how our platform works. Once you're ready, you can enroll in any of our programs directly through our website. Our support team will guide you through the entire enrollment process.
What makes PrepMe.Pro different from other prep platforms?
We combine personalized learning with expert mentorship and university partnerships. Our technology-first approach ensures you get the best of both worlds: structured courses and personalized guidance. We've helped 1,700+ college students achieve their goals, and we're dedicated to your success.
Do you offer support for international students?
Yes! While we're based in India, we serve students globally. Our online platform is accessible from anywhere, and our team can support students across different time zones. For international queries, please reach out to partnerships@prepme.pro with your specific requirements.
