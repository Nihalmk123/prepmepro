Frequently Asked Questions

Which exam should I start with?

KPSC SDA/FDA is ideal if you're new to competitive exams — it has monthly notifications and shorter syllabus. UPSC requires more preparation time. Banking exams are great for speed-focused students. We'll recommend based on your background and goals.

Can I prepare for multiple exams?

Yes! Many students prepare for KPSC while building UPSC foundation. Banking + SSC overlap in aptitude. We can create a personalized roadmap combining exams based on your schedule.

What if I'm a complete beginner?

Our curriculum starts from basics. Live classes, recorded content, and daily drills are designed for zero background. Monthly 1-on-1 mentorship helps you settle in. Most of our successful students were beginners.

Do you provide Kannada medium?

Our live classes and materials are in English with Kannada explanations where needed. We provide Kannada translations for key concepts. Contact us for specific exam Kannada medium availability.

What's the refund policy?

Full refund within 7 days if unsatisfied. 50% refund up to 14 days. After 14 days, no refund but you keep lifetime access to purchased content.
