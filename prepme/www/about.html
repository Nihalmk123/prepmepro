Our Story

Our Journey

From identifying an education access gap to building a technology-first learning platform for ambitious students.

01

The Gap We Saw

PrepMe.Pro’s founding team observed a major challenge: millions of ambitious students in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities had little or no access to quality coaching, expert guidance, and structured pathways.

02

The Foundation

This insight led to the creation of Shree Honnakirana Academy LLP, built on a simple principle — great education should never be limited by geography or socioeconomic background.

03

The Launch

In 2026, PrepMe.Pro was launched as a technology-first, hybrid learning platform created to democratize career education for students across India.

04

Where We Stand Today

Today, PrepMe.Pro operates at the intersection of education and technology, helping students prepare for competitive exams, study abroad opportunities, and life beyond academics.
